Hi, I'm Aradhya 👋

I'm a student who genuinely enjoys the process of building things — not just for grades or assignments, but because I like seeing an idea turn into something people can actually click, play, and use. That curiosity has led me down the path of web development and interactive projects.

Most recently, I've been on a bit of a game-building streak — putting together browser-based games from scratch, working through the logic first (rules, win conditions, computer opponents with different difficulty levels) before polishing how they look and feel. I like that discipline: get it working, then make it beautiful.

Outside of code, I'm still figuring things out like most students are — collecting skills, certificates, and small wins one project at a time, and slowly building a body of work I'm proud to share.

"Build first for yourself. Polish it enough to share with everyone else."
